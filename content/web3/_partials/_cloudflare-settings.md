Once your gateway becomes [active](/web3/reference/gateway-status/), you can customize the Cloudflare settings associated with your hostname.

Since your traffic is automatically proxied through Cloudflare, you customize your website settings to take advantage of various [security, performance, and reliability](/fundamentals/get-started/concepts/how-cloudflare-works/#benefits) benefits.
