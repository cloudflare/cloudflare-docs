Cipher suites are a combination of ciphers used to negotiate security settings during the [SSL/TLS handshake](https://www.cloudflare.com/learning/ssl/what-happens-in-a-tls-handshake/) (and therefore separate from the [SSL/TLS protocol](/ssl/reference/protocols/)).
