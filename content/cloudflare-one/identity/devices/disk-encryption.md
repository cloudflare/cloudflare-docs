# Disk Encryption (beta)

<details>
<summary>Feature availability</summary>
<div>

| Status | Operating Systems | [WARP mode required](/cloudflare-one/connections/connect-devices/warp/#warp-client-modes) | [Zero Trust plans](https://www.cloudflare.com/teams-pricing/) |
| --- | ----------------- | --------- | ---- |
| Beta | macOS, Windows, Linux | WARP with Gateway | All plans |

</div>
</details>

The Disk Encryption device posture attribute ensures that disks are encrypted on a device.

To enable the Disk Encryption check:

1.  On the Zero Trust Dashboard, navigate to **My Team** > **Devices** > **Device posture**.
2.  Click **+Add**.
3.  Select **Disk Encryption**.
4.  Enter a descriptive name for the check.
5.  Select your operating system.
6.  Toggle on the **Enable Disk Encryption** switch.
7.  Click **Save**.

Your device posture attribute is now visible on the **Device posture** page.
