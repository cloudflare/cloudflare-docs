# Enable the Consent Management Platform (CMP)

1. Log in to the [Cloudflare dashboard](https://dash.cloudflare.com/login), and select your account and website.
2. Go to **Zaraz** > **Consent**.
3. Toggle **Enable Consent Management** on.
4. Fill the information pertaining to **Administrator identity**, **Company street address**, and **Administrator email address**. Without this information the consent modal will not work. This is to ensure GDPR compliance.
5. Select **Add new Purpose**. Give your new Purpose a name, choose the Legal basis and give it a description.
6. In **Assign purpose to tools**, locate the tool you are assigning a purpose to. Select a purpose from the dropdown menu and choose one of the Purposes to assign it to your tool. Do this for all your tools.
