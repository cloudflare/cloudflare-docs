# What does Scrape Shield do?



## Overview

**Scrape Shield** is a free app in theÂ Cloudflare dashboard that protects your site's content.

To access Scrape Shield:

1.  Log into the [Cloudflare dashboard](https://dash.cloudflare.com/login) and select your account and domain.
2.  Go to **Scrape Shield**.

ScrapeShield has the following features:

1.  [Email obfuscation](https://support.cloudflare.com/hc/articles/200170016): Scramble e-mail addresses for bots to prevent email spam
2.  [Hotlink protection](https://support.cloudflare.com/hc/articles/200170026): Prevent bandwidth abuse
3.  [Server side excludes](https://support.cloudflare.com/hc/articles/200170036): hide specific content on your site from suspicious visitors
