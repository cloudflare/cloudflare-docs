# Mac setup instructions

## Block malware

### IPv4
1. Go to System Preferences. You can find it by pressing Command+Space on your Mac and typing System Preferences.
1. Click on the icon called Network.
1. Click Advanced.
1. Select the DNS tab. Remove any IP addresses that may be already listed and in their place add the following IP addresses:
    * **1.1.1.2**
    * **1.0.0.2**
1. Click OK.
1. Click Apply.

### IPv6
1. Go to System Preferences. You can find it by pressing Command+Space on your Mac and typing System Preferences.
1. Click on the icon called Network.
1. Click Advanced.
1. Select the DNS tab. Remove any IP addresses that may be already listed and in their place add the IPv6 address we listed below:
    * **2606:4700:4700::1112**
    * **2606:4700:4700::1002**
1. Click OK.
1. Click Apply.
