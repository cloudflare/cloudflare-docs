# Policy management

To manage your network policies, navigate to **Policies** > **Network**.

## Create a network policy

1. Under **Policies** > **Network**, click **Create a policy**.
1. Enter a policy name and an optional description.
1. Next, [build an expression](/policies/filtering/network-policies#expressions) by choosing a selector, operator, and value to match against.
1. Select an Action to perform on network traffic that matches the expression.
1. Click **Create policy**.

Your network policy has now been created. 

## Edit a network policy

1. Under **Policies** > **Network**, find the policy you want to edit.
1. Click **Edit**.
1. Make the desired changes to the policy configuration.
1. Click **Save policy**.

Your changes will now be reflected in your network policy.

## Delete a network policy

1. Under **Policies** > **Network**, find the policy you want to edit.
1. Click **Delete**.
1. A pop-up message will ask you to confirm your decision to delete the policy. Click **Delete**.

Your policy has now been deleted.
