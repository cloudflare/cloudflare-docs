# Android Mobile Client

## Installation Requirements
<table border="1">
    <tr>
        <th>OS Ver</th>
        <td>5.0+</td>
    </tr>
</table>

## Steps to download
[Click here](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.cloudflare.onedotonedotonedotone) to download from the Google Play store, or search for "1.1.1.1: Faster & Safer Internet"


## How to remove the application
1. Find the application on the home screen.
1. Touch and hold on the application tile.
1. Tap **Remove App**.
1. Select **Delete App**.
