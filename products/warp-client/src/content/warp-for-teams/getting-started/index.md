# Getting started

1. Make sure [Cloudflare Access](https://developers.cloudflare.com/access/getting-started/access-setup) and [Cloudflare Gateway](https://developers.cloudflare.com/gateway/getting-started/onboarding-gateway/) are set up.
1. Have your auth domain re
1. In your [Teams dashboard](http://dash.teams.cloudflare.com/), navigate to **My Teams** > **Devices**.
1. Under **Device Settings**, create a device enrollment [policy](https://developers.cloudflare.com/access/getting-started/policies) to define who can connect a device to your organization.
1. Set up Gateway [policies](https://developers.cloudflare.com/gateway/reference/policy) for your organization.

<Aside>

If you are configuring <a href="https://developers.cloudflare.com/gateway/getting-started/configuring-http-policy">HTTP policies</a>, deploy the <a href="https://developers.cloudflare.com/gateway/connecting-to-gateway/install-cloudflare-cert">root certificate</a> to your devices. 

</Aside>

1. Verify your devices meet the Cloudflare WARP [installations requirements](/setting-up/requirements).
1. Follow the setup instructions for deployment:
    * [Windows](/teams/Windows-Teams/)
    * [macOS](/teams/macOS-Teams/)
    * [iOS](/teams/iOS-Teams/)
    * [Android](/teams/android-Teams/)
