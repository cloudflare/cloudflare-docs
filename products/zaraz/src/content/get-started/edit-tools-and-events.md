# Edit tools and events

1. Log in to the [Cloudflare dashboard](https://dash.cloudflare.com/login), and select your account and website. 
1. Go to **Zaraz**.
1. Under **Third-party tools**, locate your tool and click **Edit**.

On this page you will be able to edit settings related to the tool, add extra events, and edit existing ones. To edit an existing event, click its name.

## Enable or disable a tool

1. Log in to the [Cloudflare dashboard](https://dash.cloudflare.com/login), and select your account and website. 
1. Go to **Zaraz**.
1. Under **Third-party tools**, locate your tool and click the **Enabled** toggle.

## Delete a tool

1. Log in to the [Cloudflare dashboard](https://dash.cloudflare.com/login), and select your account and website. 
1. Go to **Zaraz**.
1. Under **Third-party tools**, locate your tool and click **Delete**.
