# Console Connect Users

1. Click **+** to create a new connection.

2. From the **Choose source port** dropdown, choose your source port.

![](../static/console-connect-port.png)

3. Select **Another Company** and then enter **Cloudflare**.

![](../static/console-connect-search.png)

4. For **Rate Limit (speed)**, choose your rate limit.
5. Select **Next**.

![](../static/console-connect-speed.png)

5. Review the pricing information, and when you are done, click **Save Payment**.

6. Cloudflare will accept the connection and your Account Team will get in touch with next steps for BGP session establishment
