# Review and save a Waiting Room

Select the **Review** tab in the **Create Waiting Room** page to review the settings for your waiting room, preview how the Waiting Room will appear to visitors, and save your waiting room.



![Waiting Room Review](../../../static/wr-dashboard-edit-review.png)

To modify settings or edit the waiting room HTML, click **Settings** or **Customization**.

Click **Save** to save your waiting room configuration.
The list of waiting rooms displays in the **Waiting Rooms** dashboard.
